How to play
Goal: complete every ★ required directive by its deadline. Reach the prestige threshold for a MAJOR win.

Buildings are free but Earth only delivers a few per tier each turn (the counts in “Earth delivery”). Nothing is stockpiled — each turn every building turns input flows into output flows; spare output feeds your active directives or is lost.

Placement matters. Mines need their deposit tile. Solar is stronger toward the sunward (left) edge. Heat plants (Smelter/Foundry/Reactor/…) run better next to a Radiator. Reactors irradiate neighbours — keep Habitats away (or in a lava tube ~). A building gets a bonus per adjacent input producer, so cluster your supply chains.

Workers come from Habitats (which need food/water/O₂/power). Every other building needs staffing. Demolish to rework a full map (free this turn; refunds the slot if placed this turn).

Directive rewards raise build-rate / unlock buildings — that’s how you grow.
